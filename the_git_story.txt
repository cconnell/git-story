... said the goblin under a bridge in the northern region of nova scotia. He had long been banished to the shadows,
seeking refuge in the shade and hiding from the beautiful people still allowed to bask in the glorious sunlight. The
lack of vitamin D had withered his body to near nothingness. Or perhaps a side effect from the lines. "Try a third time,"
he chants over and over. "Try a third time." Always under the bridge, always in the shadows, "try a third time." Fin.
